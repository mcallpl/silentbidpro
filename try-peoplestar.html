You're among the first real people to run through Silent Bid Buddy end to end. Follow the lots below in order — if anything breaks, looks off, or a text doesn't show up, that's exactly what we want to hear.

How to use this

Go through it together — same room or a phone call. Most steps you each do on your own phone; in one step you'll bid against each other. Give it about 20–30 minutes, and leave your notes in the feedback box at the bottom.

The walkthrough

Six lots, in order

Tap a lot number to check it off as you go.

Lot1

Sign in

  • Tap Sign In to Bid.
  • Enter your name, phone number, and email.
  • You'll get a 6-digit code by text — type it in.
Check: Did the text arrive quickly, and did the code let you in?
Lot2

Browse the items

  • Look through the auction items and open a few.
Check: Do the photos, descriptions, prices, and "time remaining" look right and read well on your phone?
Lot3

Place your first bid

  • Pick an item and place a bid.
Check: Did it confirm the bid, and are you now shown as the high bidder?
Lot4
★ The fun part — do this together

Bid against each other

  • One person bids on an item. Then someone else bids higher on the same item.
  • The person who was leading should be told they were outbid (a text and/or a notification).
  • Keep going — take turns outbidding each other on one or two items.
Check: Did the outbid alerts arrive, and did the high bidder always update to the right person?
Lot5

Watch & track

  • Watch (favorite) an item you're not bidding on.
  • Open the My Bids & Watching menu.
Check: Does it correctly list what you bid on and what you're watching, with the right status — Winning or Outbid?
Lot6

Win & check out

  • On one item, let one person be the final high bidder (we'll close that item so you can test payment).
  • The winner opens My Bids → the won item → Checkout.
  • Pay with the test card 4242 4242 4242 4242, any future expiry, any CVC and ZIP.
Check: Did checkout go through and show a confirmation or receipt?
